About Me

Before coming to codefellows I was attending college and working in customer service, I took a class in java at my college and enjoyed it so much that when COVID hit I decided to take a break and get a coding certificate. My area of study in school was physics, and I'd like to work in research someday, so it seemed only reasonable to branch out into coding. I'd like to use my newfound skills in large scale data processing in the future and to study things that are too small or too large to work with without the help of computer science. I have a lot of experience working in teams and mediating groups of people, and I love getting to know people and learning how to work with their strengths and weaknesses. I am very excited to use these team building skills in the world of tech.
