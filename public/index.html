About Me

My name is Kaya and I am a web developer who specializes in C#. I have experience working with teams and am skilled at mediating groups of varying size, and I appreciate getting know to people and learning how to work best with their strengths and weaknesses. My experience in customer service taught me to read people's emotions, which makes me excellent at understanding the needs of a consumer or those on my team. I love coding because I love problem solving and working with data in a team environment. I'm really interested in research and being on the cutting edge of whatever I'm doing, so I'm looking for a company that can get me working along side researchers, and trying new or even impossible things. I hope to bring my skill and passion to a company that values challenging their employees, and giving back to their community.
